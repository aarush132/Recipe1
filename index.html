Tikka Masala Prawn Recipe

Enjoy these delicious aromatic prawns with a rich goleden paste.
Forget a take away and tuck into a quick and effortless meal that will please the whole family.

Ingredients

Masala Marinade

2 tbsp. Pathaks tikka masala paste

2 tbsp. Coriander leaves

½ cup Yogurt

Ginger and Garlic paste 2tsp

¼ tsp. turmeric powder

¼ tsp. Garam masala powder

¼ tsp. Jeera powder

½ tsp. Coriander powder

1 tsp. Soya sauce

1 tbsp. Cooking Oil

Salt to taste

Other

450g Prawns

¼ Lemon

Method

1. Mix all the masala marinade ingredients together and cook in a pan on medium flame until oil separates.

2. Let the masala cool down for 10 minutes.

3. Clean and divine the prawns then mix well with the masala.

4. span class="white">Cook the prawns in the frying pan until pink and tender. Once cooked squeeze lemon on top and stir.
